## About Me
> I'm Gali, a Master's student in the Faculty of Chemistry at the Weizmann Institute of Science. I hold a BSc in Materials Engineering and Chemistry from the Technion, where my final project focused on mapping oxygen evolution reaction in Ni-hydroxide electrodes.
